How does it work?

1. Create a page

Sign up as a writer, create a “Page,” and then embed the page into your existing stories as a friendly button to guide readers to your page.

2. Let ideas grow

On your page, readers can create “Visions” of your next topics and pledge funds. They can also back each other's visions to help good ideas grow.

3. Collect pledges, then publish

Be inspired and start crafting a new story. Get support for your work by pressing the “Collect Pledges” button on your page. Then, publish* the story when done. Enjoy being loved by your audience!

* Envisionor values openness—your story needs to be published for everyone (not just the Medium members) in order to receive pledges. So, everyone in the world can read and benefit from your story.
