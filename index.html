How does it work?

1. Create a vision

Sign up as a reader, create a Vision of the next topic to be written by your favorite writer and pledge an amount to pay if it comes true. Inform the writer your vision by responding to her existing stories.

2. Amplify what matters

The writer reviews your vision and adds it into her Page, so other readers can see your vision and back it too. Similarly, you can back other people's visions to amplify what matters.

3. Enjoy your read

Get notified when the writer starts realizing your vision. Offer your pledged funds to help her make it, and the writer will acknowledge your support in the new story. Voila—the unique perspectives, insights or voice you helped made public!

* Envisionor collects an additional 3% + $0.3 payment processing fee per pledge. For example, if you pledged $10 to a vision. You will be charged $10 + ($10 * 3%) + $0.3 = $10.6 when the pledge is collected. You can reduces fees by batching charges at the beginning of each month.
