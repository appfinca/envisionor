How it works

1. Create a page

Sign up and create a Page. Guid your audience to the page by showing its URL in your existing videos or by adding a link in end screens or cards.

2. Let ideas grow

On your page, fans create Visions of the scenes they want to see in your next video and optionally pledge funds. They can also back each other's visions.

3. Collect pledges & film

Be inspired, and start realizing the visions you like. Get support for your work by collecting the pledges of the visions. Voila! Your next awesome video!

Why Envisionor?

Informative

Envisionor is not just a crowdfunding platform. It’s where new ideas grow! By accepting visions, you understand your audience better, which helps you create engaging videos.

Prepaid

You can capture the pledged funds as soon as you begin realizing a vision. Your filming expense can be covered in the first place, giving you a lower risk to try something new.

Organized

Stop waisting time on categorizing comments and taking notes by yourself. Envisionor automatically organizes and priorities ideas for you. Bye bye, comments hell. Hello, neat visions!

Natural

Feeling awkward to ask for money from your audience? Why not ask for visions instead? Your fans will be eager to tell you what they want to see and even make pledges in order to get your attention.
